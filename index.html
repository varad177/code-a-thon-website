Round 2: 24-Hour Online Hackathon

Date: March 29, 2025

1. Platform: The hackathon will be hosted on Discord. All team members must join the server.
2. Duration: A continuous 24-hour coding challenge.
3. Monitoring: Teams must stay active on Discord. Random check-ins and screen-sharing sessions will be conducted. Any rule violations may result in disqualification.

2. Evaluation Criteria: Projects will be judged based on functionality, innovation, technical complexity, and presentation clarity. Teams should effectively communicate their problem-solving approach and execution.

3. Results & Prizes: Winners will be announced after all presentations. Prizes and certificates will be awarded at the college, recognizing the best innovations and technical excellence.
